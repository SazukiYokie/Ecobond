The Science

The Zero-Heat Formula

1. Recycle

Local farm waste (like rice husks) is burned entirely into Bio-Ash, creating a natural silica base.

2. Reuse

Discarded plastic trash is collected and shredded into tiny pieces, acting as a flexible, durable filler.

3. Reduce

Mixed with cold water and local Lime (កំបោរស). It cures into a rock-solid patch. Zero industrial heat. Zero toxic fumes.
